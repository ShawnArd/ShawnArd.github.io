Welcome!

I am an aspiring full-stack web developer and a recent graduate of Northwestern's Full-Stack immersive program. Currently I am working at The Difference Engine, a Non-Profit Organization (NPO) that builds websites for charities and other NPO's. My current stack involves utilizing a React front-end with a Python/Django based Backend.


I've always had an interest in Civics and technology. During my time at Penn State I studied Biological Engineering and became the IT Director for the Council of Commonwealth Student Governments. I cherished my time as IT Director, learning basic Php, HTML, and CSS. After graduation I worked at the University of Chicago as a Research Assistant, but found little love running repeatative experiments and chasing elusive grants. I yearned for the sense of creativity and freedom programming offered me and after two years decided to head back into coding. I started with Codecademy, then Codecademy Pro and Free Code Camp, and when I had prepared enough I left my career in research to persue my passion at Northwestern.
